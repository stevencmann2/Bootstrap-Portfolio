About Me

My name is Steven Mann, an aspiring web developer in Houston, TX. I graduated from St. Edwards University with a bachelor's degree in Business Administration. Since then I have spent my time working in Houston across various fields before ultimately deciding to switch carrer paths. Currently, I am attending a coding bootcamp through the University of Texas. I hope o become a web developer through the completion of this bootcamp and to be ready to launch a new career.

When I am not working on my career, I enjoy writing, recording and performing music across the state of Texas. I perform in several local bands and enjoy playing genres from country and americana to blues and rock. I have opened for acts such as Kyle Hollingsworth, Dumpstafunk, Naughty Professor, George Porter Jr., Organ Freeman and The Texas Gentlemen. I also enjoy outdoor activities such as biking, fishing, and camping. On my most recent camping trip, I traveled to Lost Maples just outside of San Antonio, spending four days and nights in the remote Texas hillcountry.
